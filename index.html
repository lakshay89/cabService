ABOUT OUR COMPANY

Welcome to Manona Dham Cab Service

Your trusted travel partner for sacred journeys to Manona Dham, Bareilly. Experience comfort, safety, and devotion as you visit one of India most revered spiritual destinations. The legend dates back to the Mahabharata era, with Shyam Baba (Barbarik) being the grandson of Bhim. Barbarik’s ultimate sacrifice was recognized by Lord Krishna, who promised him eternal worship as Khatu Shyam Ji in the Kaliyug. The temple became widely known after Mahant Omendra Chauhan Ji’s divine vision in 2019, leading to miraculous happenings and the temple’s rise as a place of spiritual healing and faith.
